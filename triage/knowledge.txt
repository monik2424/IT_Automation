=== IT Operations Standard Operating Procedures ===

ISSUE: High CPU Usage
CAUSE: Runaway process or memory leak.
ACTION: Identify top process with 'top' command. Kill with 'kill -9 <pid>'. Monitor for 30 minutes.

ISSUE: Disk Space Critical
CAUSE: Log files, temp files, or large dumps accumulating.
ACTION: Run 'du -sh /*' to find large directories. Clear /tmp and old logs in /var/log. Alert if over 90%.

ISSUE: Apache / Service Down
CAUSE: Crash due to config error, memory, or port conflict.
ACTION: Check 'systemctl status apache2'. Review /var/log/apache2/error.log. Restart with 'systemctl restart apache2'.

ISSUE: VPN Connection Timeout
CAUSE: Expired certificate, misconfigured client, or firewall rule change.
ACTION: Check VPN gateway logs. Verify SSL certificate expiry date. Confirm firewall allows UDP 1194. Restart VPN service.

ISSUE: Unknown / Generic
CAUSE: Unclear — needs investigation.
ACTION: Collect logs from affected system. Escalate to Tier 2 with full log dump.
